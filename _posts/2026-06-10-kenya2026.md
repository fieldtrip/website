### 10 June 2026

On Monday Robert presented an EEG/ERP workshop in Nairobi (Kenya) organized by [ALMA](https://almaafrica.org/) prior to the [Institute for Human Development International Conference](https://www.aku.edu/ihd/Pages/ihd-conference.aspx). The one-day workshop consisted of lectures and a live demonstration of an EEG and ERP recording with resting state and an oddball task, and the analysis of the EEG data.
