### 01 December, 2025

FieldTrip version [20251201](http://github.com/fieldtrip/fieldtrip/releases/tag/20251201) has been released. It contains several small improvements with respect to the last posted release (20250928).

Some highlights are:
- update of the implementation of SSS, now also allowing for movement correction in **[ft_denoise_sss](/reference/ft_denoise_sss)**.
- improved support for the use of custom preprocessing functions in **[ft_preprocessing](/reference/ft_preprocessing)**.
  
See [GitHub](https://github.com/fieldtrip/fieldtrip/compare/20251125...20251201) for the detailed list of updates.
